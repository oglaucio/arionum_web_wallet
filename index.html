Disclaimer

MyArionum is a web-based interface that allows you to use Arionum without running a full Arionum node. However, because this convenience comes at a cost: it is extremely difficult for MyArionum to securely deliver its code to your browser. This means that there is considerable risk in using MyArionum for large amounts!

It is recommended that you treat MyArionum as you would treat your actual wallet, and not store very large amounts in it. For long-term storage of Arionum you should create a cold wallet using Arionum Address or similar.

Take Note of your Private Login Key!

Below this you will find your thirteen word "Private Login Key". Keeping this secure and private is very important, as it is the only way that you will be able to login to your MyArionum account. As we don't store your private login key on the server there is no way to recover it if it is lost! Your private login key can also never be changed, and if it is stolen or otherwise compromised, you will have to move your funds to a new account with a new private login key. Therefore, it is best that you backup your private login key by writing it down, perhaps obscuring it as part of a poem or letter, and storing it in multiple safe and secure places.
